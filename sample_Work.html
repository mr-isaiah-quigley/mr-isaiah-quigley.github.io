Lesson 3: The Olympics		 Three lesson plans were created (but not taught) as part of a Unit Plan. This Unit Plan was a group project, and it focused on Ancient Greece. My portion of the project covered the history of Ancient Greece (SOL 3.3). My first lesson discusses the architecture used by the Ancient Greeks with a focus on columns and the Parthenon and their influence on modern architecture. My second lesson discusses system of government used by the Ancient Greeks (Direct Democracy) and how the U.S.'s system (Representative Democracy) is based on this form. The final lesson considers differences between the ancient and modern olympics.
